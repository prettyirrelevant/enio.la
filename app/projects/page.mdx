# Projects

Some things I've built over the years.

## [<Guessx/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/guessx 'Guessx')

A real-time multiplayer guessing game for 2-20 players. Three modes: identify songs from audio previews, landmarks from photos, or actors from headshots. Points awarded by speed with streak bonuses.

<TechTags tags={['TypeScript', 'Next.js', 'Convex', 'Tailwind CSS']} />

## [<Knot/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/knot 'Knot')

A real-time 1v1 tic-tac-toe game.

<TechTags tags={['TypeScript', 'Next.js', 'Convex', 'Zustand']} />

## [<Kizami/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/kizami 'Kizami')

A Rust service for block-by-timestamp lookups across 30+ EVM chains, with reorg-safe ingestion and in-memory caching.

<TechTags tags={['Rust', 'Fjall']} />

## [<Bridgebloc/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/bridgebloc 'Bridgebloc')

A cross-chain USDC bridge using Circle CCTP and Polygon LxLy. Built the blockchain indexer, integrated Circle's APIs for cross-chain swaps. Won $9,000 at the DevX EMEA hackathon out of 300+ submissions.

<TechTags tags={['Python', 'Django', 'Solidity', 'Circle CCTP']} />

## [<Wakaru/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/wakaru 'Wakaru')

A client-side bank statement analyzer with visual analytics and natural language queries. No data leaves the browser.

<TechTags tags={['TypeScript', 'Next.js', 'Tailwind CSS']} />

## [<Decodify/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/decodify 'Decodify')

A browser extension that decodes Etherscan transactions into a human-readable format using Rotki's transaction decoder.

<TechTags tags={['TypeScript', 'Chrome Extension']} />

## [<WrappedNaira/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/wrapped-naira 'Wrapped Naira')

A proof-of-concept fiat-to-crypto ramp with a P2P exchange mechanism for on/off-ramping between Naira and an ERC-20 stablecoin. Won a prize at Scroll's v0rtex hackathon and was [highlighted by Scroll](https://x.com/Scroll_ZKP/status/1788017634862370925).

<TechTags tags={['Python', 'Django', 'Solidity']} />

## [<Neuron/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/final-year-project 'Neuron')

My undergraduate final year project: a student attendance system using Bluetooth Low Energy with an ESP32 microcontroller, a mobile app, and an authentication server.

<TechTags tags={['C++', 'ESP32', 'React Native', 'TypeScript', 'Python', 'Django']} />

## [<Waakye/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/waakye 'Waakye')

A playlist converter that moves your music between Spotify, YouTube Music, and Deezer, handling track matching and preserving playlist metadata.

<TechTags tags={['Python', 'Django', 'React']} />

## [<Gistrunner/>](https://github.com/prettyirrelevant/gistrunner 'Gistrunner')

A sandbox environment for executing code snippets directly from GitHub Gists, with support for multiple languages and isolated execution.

<TechTags tags={['Go', 'Docker', 'Next.js']} />
